What is PRO?

Project ReachOut is a dynamic non-profit organization committed to fostering social consciousness among youth. Through three distinct avenues - Green Way for environmental awareness, Animal Way for advocating animal welfare, and Special Way for supporting specially-abled children. Project ReachOut strives to empower young individuals to become agents of positive change in their communities. By providing educational resources, organizing impactful events, and fostering empathy and inclusivity, Project ReachOut aims to cultivate a generation of socially aware and compassionate leaders dedicated to making a difference in the world.
